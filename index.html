Postdoctoral Fellow in Cosmology

University of Rome, Tor Vergata, Italy

Welcome to my webpage! I am a research fellow specializing in astrophysics and cosmology at the University of Rome, Tor Vergata. I successfully obtained my Ph.D. in Astrophysics and Cosmology with honors from SISSA in Trieste, Italy. My research revolves around several fascinating areas, including the analysis of Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) data, Dark Energy phenomenology, the application of Machine Learning techniques to Cosmology and Astrophysics, and Data Science. Currently, I am actively collaborating with SA, SO, and LiteBIRD.
